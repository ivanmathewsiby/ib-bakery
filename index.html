Our Story

A Legacy of Taste
in Thodupuzha

Indian Bakery has a proud history of 45 years. Founded by Mr. V. T. Joseph, located in the heart of Thodupuzha town, we blend traditional Kerala recipes with modern baking techniques.

From melt-in-the-mouth Plum Cakes to crispy evening puffs and legendary faloodas — every item is crafted with love and finest ingredients.
